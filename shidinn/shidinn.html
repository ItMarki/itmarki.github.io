Shidinn subpage/希頂分頁

Shidinn (full name: 希頂字母新華語, lit. the New Chinese Language of the Shidinn Alphabet, Shidinn: xdi8 Aho FHLmA) is a constructed language made by a Zhihu linguist and pseudoscientist under the pseudonym Huang Quefei.

希頂語（全稱：希頂字母新華語，希頂語： xdi8 Aho FHLmA）是一個由一名知乎語言學家和民間科學家（化名：黃雀飛）創造的人工語言。
